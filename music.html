Who is Lana del Rey

Lana Del Rey is an American singer, songwriter, and record producer known for her cinematic and melancholic style. Born Elizabeth Woolridge Grant on June 21, 1985, in New York City, she gained prominence with her 2011 debut single "Video Games" and her subsequent albums, including "Born to Die" and "Lust for Life." Her music often explores themes of nostalgia, romance, and American culture, and she is celebrated for her distinctive voice and evocative lyrics.
